Percival Baxter

A Great Maine Conservationist, Governor, and Philanthropist

Percival Baxter was an American politician and philanthropist from Maine. Percival was the son of a cannery owner and mayor of Portland, James Baxter. Percival served as the 53rd Governor of Maine from 1921 to 1925. Percival Donated over 300 square miles of wilderness to the state of Maine under a covenant that it be preserved for the people of Maine In perpetuity.
